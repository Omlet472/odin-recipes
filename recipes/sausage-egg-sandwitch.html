Sheet Pan Sausage Egg Sandwiches

Description

Start your morning the easy way with these sheet pan sausage egg sandwiches. They are perfect for meal prep or busy mornings, freeze well and are a great grab-and-go breakfast sandwich that you can customize to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 12 frozen, fully-cooked sausage patties, thawed
  • 12 english muffins, split and toasted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 10x15-inch rimmed baking sheet.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well combined. Pour egg mixture into the prepared baking sheet, spreading evenly.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are mostly set, 12 to 15 minutes.
  4. Arrange sausage patties evenly over the eggs in a 3x4 pattern. Sprinkle cheese evenly over the top.
  5. Return baking sheet to the oven and bake until eggs are fully set and cheese is melted, 6 to 8 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly. Cut eggs into 12 even squares.
  7. Assemble sandwiches by placing one egg-and-sausage square on each toasted English muffin.
